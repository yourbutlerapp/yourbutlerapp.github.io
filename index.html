How it works

Smart savings,
automatically.

Your Butler runs quietly in the background — matching your household list to real weekly deals so you never have to manually hunt for savings again.

01
🏠
Tell us your household
Add your household members, pick your usual stores, and build your list in minutes. Your Butler learns your patterns from day one — the more you use it, the smarter it gets.
02
🔍
We match your list to deals
Every week, 3,300+ real deals are tracked across 15+ store banners. Your Butler automatically matches what your household actually buys to what's genuinely on sale — no flyer-flipping required.
03
🛒
Shop smarter, earn rewards
Run your grocery trip with Your Butler open. Verify deals, check items off, and earn Butler Points redeemable for real rewards. Every run makes your household smarter about spending.
Features

Built for real
Canadian households.

Not just a grocery list. A complete household savings engine — designed for the way Canadian families actually shop and live.

Butler Price Match

Deal matching from your trusted local grocery and essentials stores — automatically, every week. Your list items are compared against real weekly specials so you always know where to shop for the best price.

3,300+ weekly dealsReal savings
Butler Pulse

Powered by 48 household consumption benchmarks and growing community data, Butler Pulse learns your household's rhythm and alerts you before you run out of the things that matter most.

48 consumption benchmarksSmart alerts
Butler Health

Track medications, manage appointments, and stay stocked on health supplies — all in one place. Designed for families managing their own health needs or caring for a loved one.

Coming soon
Butler Sync

Invite your partner, family members, or caregivers. Everyone shares the same list, tasks, and savings in real time — so no one is ever working from an outdated list again.

Real-time syncSmart alerts
Butler Points

Every deal verified, every grocery run completed, every item checked off earns Butler Points. Redeem for scratch cards with real prizes — bonus points, gift cards, and partner offers. The more your household uses Your Butler, the more you earn.
