Common questions

How does the 14-day free trial work?
Download the app, tap upgrade, and you'll be offered a 14-day free trial through the App Store or Google Play. No charge until the trial ends. Cancel anytime before then and you won't be billed anything.
Do I need a credit card for the free plan?
No. The free plan requires no payment information. You only provide billing details if you choose to start a premium trial through the App Store or Google Play.
How does Butler's AI deal matching actually work?
Butler scans thousands of weekly deals from local Canadian store banners and intelligently cross-references them against the items on your household list. No manual searching — the AI does the matching automatically every week.
What happens to my data if I cancel premium?
Your lists, health entries, and household data stay exactly where they are. You move back to the free plan limits — nothing is deleted. Upgrade again anytime to restore premium features instantly.
How smart is Butler Pulse's AI?
Butler Pulse starts with 48 Canadian household consumption benchmarks and calibrates over time to your specific household — your family size, purchase frequency, and verified consumption patterns. The longer you use it, the more accurate your predictions become.
Are the health features private and secure?
Butler stores health entries as user-defined text reminders — like you'd use any reminder app. We never share health data with advertisers or third parties. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
